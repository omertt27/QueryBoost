Welcome to QueryBoost

You'll get smarter AI answers from this moment on — without changing how you type.

How it works
1

You type normally

No templates, no pre-prompts, no copy-pasting. Just type into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity as you always have.

2

QueryBoost detects your intent

Before your query is sent, QueryBoost classifies it — code, explanation, how-to, analysis, creative, and more — using a weighted signal engine.

3

A structured wrapper is injected

The query is silently rewritten with precise output instructions tailored to the intent. The AI never surfaces these instructions in its response.

4

You get a noticeably better answer

A small toast confirms the boost type. Click Preview to see what was added. That's it.
